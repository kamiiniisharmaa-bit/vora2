Privacy Policy

Your data, treated with the same care as our craft — transparent, minimal, and always respectful.

Last revised: June 11, 2026 | VORA Atelier Edition

1. Our commitment to privacy

At VORA (“we”, “us”, “our”), we believe that privacy is a form of silence — essential and precious. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, make a purchase, or interact with our brand. By using VORA, you entrust us with your data, and we commit to handling it with the highest standards of transparency and security.

We align our practices with GDPR, CCPA, and other global privacy frameworks, ensuring that your rights are respected no matter where you are located.

2. Information we collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with an exceptional experience and fulfill your orders. The table below summarizes the categories of personal data we process:

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Identity & contactName, email address, shipping address, phone numberOrder fulfillment, shipping updates, customer support
Payment informationCredit card details, PayPal account (processed via secure third-party gateways)Transaction processing and fraud prevention (we do not store full card numbers)
Account credentialsLogin email, password (encrypted)Account management, wishlist, order history
Usage & device dataIP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, referral sourceSite analytics, performance improvement, personalized recommendations
CommunicationsEmail correspondence, chat messages, survey responsesCustomer care, feedback collection, product improvements

We do not collect sensitive personal data (e.g., racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, health information) unless you voluntarily provide it through customer service interactions.

3. How we use your information

VORA uses your personal data solely for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Order processing & shipping: To fulfill your purchases, send order confirmations, and provide tracking updates.
  • Customer support: To respond to inquiries, process returns, and resolve any issues.
  • Marketing communications: With your explicit consent, we may send you curated newsletters, early access offers, and craft stories. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in our emails.
  • Site improvement: To analyze how visitors interact with our Site, enabling us to enhance user experience and product discovery.
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws, respond to lawful requests, and prevent fraud.

We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your trust is the foundation of our silent luxury ethos.

4. Cookies & tracking technologies

Our Site uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality, remember preferences, and gather analytics. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for shopping cart, checkout, and account login.
  • Functional cookies: To remember your language and region preferences.
  • Analytics cookies: Via services like Google Analytics (anonymized) to understand site traffic and behavior.
  • Advertising cookies: Only if you opt in, to show relevant VORA products on partner platforms.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect site functionality.

🍪 VORA's cookie banner appears on your first visit — you have full control to accept only necessary cookies. We respect your choice.

5. Sharing your data with third parties

We share your information only with trusted partners who help us operate our business, under strict confidentiality agreements:

  • Shipping carriers: (e.g., DHL, UPS, USPS) to deliver your orders.
  • Payment processors: (Stripe, PayPal, Shopify Payments) — they handle payment data securely and do not retain information longer than necessary.
  • IT & analytics providers: (cloud hosting, customer support software) to maintain our platform.
  • Legal authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information for marketing purposes. Any third-party service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for specified services.

6. Data retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. For example:

  • Order data is kept for 7 years to comply with tax and accounting regulations.
  • Marketing preferences are retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • Site analytics data is anonymized after 14 months.

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it. You may request deletion of your account data at any time (see Section 8).

7. Security measures

Protecting your data is integral to our craft. We implement industry-standard safeguards:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our Site is protected by SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Access controls: Only authorized VORA personnel have access to personal data, and only when necessary for their role.
  • Regular audits: We conduct security assessments and vulnerability scans.
  • Payment security: We are PCI-DSS compliant and do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.

While no system is 100% secure, we continuously update our practices to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your information.

8. Your privacy rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”): Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit how we use your data while disputes are resolved.
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: Opt out of marketing or certain types of processing.
  • Right to withdraw consent: For any processing based on your consent (e.g., marketing emails).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@vora.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

✦ California residents: Under the CCPA, you have the right to know what personal data we collect, request deletion, and opt out of the “sale” of data (note: VORA does not sell data). ✦

✦ EU/UK residents: Under GDPR, we process your data based on lawful grounds (contract, legitimate interest, or consent). ✦

9. Children's privacy

VORA does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16. Our products and services are directed toward adults. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately, and we will delete it.

10. International data transfers

VORA operates globally, and your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that adequate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for transfers from the EU/UK, and we maintain equivalent protections for all users.

11. Third-party links

Our Site may contain links to external websites (e.g., our Instagram, journal references). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The “Last revised” date at the top of this page indicates when the latest version was published. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email (if you have an account) or through a notice on our Site.
